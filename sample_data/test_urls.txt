# Sample Wikipedia URLs for Testing

## Test Cases

1. **Computer Science Pioneer**
   - URL: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alan_Turing
   - Expected: Questions about early life, WWII contributions, Enigma code, Turing machine

2. **Historical Figure**
   - URL: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marie_Curie
   - Expected: Questions about Nobel Prizes, radioactivity research, personal life

3. **Scientific Concept**
   - URL: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quantum_mechanics
   - Expected: Questions about principles, history, key scientists

4. **Geographic Location**
   - URL: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paris
   - Expected: Questions about history, landmarks, culture

5. **Technology**
   - URL: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Artificial_intelligence
   - Expected: Questions about AI history, applications, ethics

6. **Historical Event**
   - URL: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II
   - Expected: Questions about timeline, major battles, outcomes

7. **Shorter Article (for quick testing)**
   - URL: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Python_(programming_language)
   - Expected: Questions about creation, features, uses
